Investment Management

Based on ENF information management platform, we manage the investment of Photovoltaic projects, including feasibility study, due diligence, design evaluation, equipment supervision, construction supervision, acceptance inspection, post-construction monitoring and market value management.

Project Information Input Platform

Based on our industry channels, partners and O2O internet channel, the Project Information Input Platform collects basic information of projects seeking finance, making procedures for PV project development.

Evaluation Platform

The Evaluation Platform evaluates the economic and technical parameters of each project, and then grade the project from AAA to CCC, which is robust and professional to manage the investment risks.

Asset Management Platform

The Asset management Platform, made up of monitoring system and operation & maintenance system, is to carry out asset management after the projects are matched with finance.

Trading Platform

The Trading Platform will meet the needs of institutional financers and provide standardized Photovoltaic investment products by Internet Finance.
