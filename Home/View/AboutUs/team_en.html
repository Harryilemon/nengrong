Core Team

Chief Scientist

Sicheng Wang

Sicheng Wang is the Chief Scientist of ENetF. Mr Wang is well-known in PV industry and academy. From 1982, he works on PV product development, project construction, industrial standard and related law and regulation formulating. He is chairman of Chinese expert team of IEC (International Electrotechnical Commission) TC82 and was a researcher in National Development and Reform Commission before his retirement.

Chairman

Lin Wan

Lin Wan is the chairman of ENetF and also the Chinese representative of APEC (Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation) new energy expert team. Mr Wan is experienced in domestic subsidy PV programme review and worked in Ministry of Transport of the People's Republic of China for 15 years.

CEO

Yue Li

Yue Li is the CEO of ENetF and professional in solar finance. Ms Li graduated from Stanford University and has extended experience in the US and China solar power industry. She also has entrepreneurial experience in the carbon offset sector in China from 2005 to 2008.
